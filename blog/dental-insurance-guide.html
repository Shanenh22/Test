Types of Plans

PPO: Network dentists at reduced fees. Typically 100% preventive, 80% basic, 50% major, up to annual max.

HMO/DHMO: Requires primary dentist. Lower premiums, less flexibility.

Discount Plans: Not insurance. Reduced fees, no maximums or waiting periods.

Maximizing Benefits

Plans reset January 1. Schedule preventive visits early. Don't let benefits expire. We verify coverage before treatment.
