What Is Gum Disease?

Nearly half of adults over 30 have some form (CDC). Ranges from gingivitis to periodontitis with bone/tooth loss.

Warning Signs

Red/swollen gums. Bleeding. Bad breath. Receding gums. Loose teeth.

Prevention

Brush twice daily, floss daily, use mouthwash, eat well, avoid tobacco, see your dentist regularly.
