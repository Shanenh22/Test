Oral Health Education

The ADA recommends brushing twice daily for two minutes with fluoride toothpaste and flossing once per day.

Brushing Technique

Hold at a 45-degree angle to the gum line. Short, gentle strokes on all surfaces. Brush your tongue. Replace brush every 3–4 months.

Flossing Technique

Use 18 inches. Guide gently with a C-shaped motion. Use clean section for each tooth.

Additional Tips

Use antimicrobial mouthwash. Limit sugary foods. Drink fluoridated water. Visit your dentist every six months.
