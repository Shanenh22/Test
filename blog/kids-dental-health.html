Oral Health Education

Starting Early

AAPD recommends first visit by age 1. Check for decay, assess development, guide feeding practices.

Common Concerns

Baby Bottle Decay: Avoid bottles at bedtime. Thumb-Sucking: Harmless before age 4. Cavities: 20% of kids 5–11 have untreated decay (CDC). Sealants help.

Building Habits

Pea-sized fluoride toothpaste ages 3+. Supervise until age 7–8. Lead by example.
