Oral Health Education

Teeth Whitening

Best for surface stains. Several shades lighter in one visit. Most affordable option. Results last 1–3 years.

Porcelain Veneers

Best for chips, gaps, severe discoloration. Custom shells. Last 10–15+ years. Most dramatic transformation.

Dental Bonding

Best for minor chips and gaps. Tooth-colored resin. Less expensive than veneers. Lasts 5–7 years.

Invisalign

Best for alignment issues. Clear aligners over 12–18 months. Addresses root cause rather than masking.
