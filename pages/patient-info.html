Patient Resources

Information for New & Existing Patients

Welcome to Oasis Dental! We want to make your visit as smooth and comfortable as possible.

What to Bring

Arrive 15 minutes early. Bring photo ID, dental insurance card (if applicable), list of current medications, and any previous dental X-rays.

Insurance & Payment

We accept most major dental insurance plans, including PPO and DHMO. Our team is happy to verify your benefits before treatment. For patients without insurance, we offer the $99 New Patient Special.

Payment Options

Cash, check, all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, AmEx, Discover), and CareCredit financing. Payment due at time of service.

Cancellation Policy

Please provide at least 24 hours notice to reschedule or cancel, allowing us to offer the slot to another patient in need.
