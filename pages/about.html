About Oasis Dental

Meet Dr. Sara Viyapon, DMD

Dr. Saranya Viyapon, DMD, has been serving the Keller community for over 20 years. A graduate of the University of Pennsylvania and Chiang Mai University, she combines decades of clinical expertise with a gentle, patient-centered approach.

Dr. Viyapon founded Oasis Dental with a simple mission: to provide high-quality dental care in a relaxing, stress-free environment where every patient feels valued. During her free time, she enjoys traveling the world with her three daughters.

At Oasis Dental, we believe prevention is the best medicine. Our practice emphasizes patient education, early detection, and conservative treatment whenever possible. We use modern technology — including digital X-rays with reduced radiation — to deliver efficient, comfortable care.

  • University of Pennsylvania graduate
  • Two convenient locations: Keller & Fort Worth
  • Patient-centered, compassionate approach
  • Modern technology and techniques
