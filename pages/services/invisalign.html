Clear Aligner Therapy

Straighter Teeth Without Metal Braces

Invisalign uses custom-made, virtually invisible plastic aligners to gradually shift teeth into position. Unlike traditional braces, aligners are removable for eating and hygiene. Treatment typically takes 12–18 months for adults.

Each set of aligners is worn for about one to two weeks before progressing to the next set. Digital impressions and 3D treatment planning allow you to preview your results before you begin.

  • Virtually invisible — most people won't notice
  • Removable for eating and oral hygiene
  • Smooth, comfortable plastic — no metal wires
  • Fewer office visits than traditional braces
  • Treats crowding, spacing, overbite, and crossbite
