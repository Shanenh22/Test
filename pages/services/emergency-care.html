When You Need a Dentist Right Now

Dental emergencies happen without warning. The ADA recommends seeking emergency care immediately for severe toothaches, knocked-out teeth, or oral infections to prevent complications.

At Oasis Dental, we reserve same-day appointment slots for emergencies so you can receive prompt pain relief and treatment.

  • Severe toothaches and dental pain
  • Knocked-out or displaced teeth
  • Cracked, fractured, or broken teeth
  • Lost fillings, crowns, or restorations
  • Dental abscesses and infections
  • Soft tissue injuries
