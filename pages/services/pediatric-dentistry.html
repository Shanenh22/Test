Children's Dentistry

Building Healthy Smiles from the Start

The ADA and AAPD recommend children visit the dentist by their first birthday. Early visits establish good habits and allow early detection of potential issues.

Our team creates a warm, welcoming environment where children feel safe. We explain procedures in age-appropriate language and make each visit positive.

  • First visit and well-baby dental exams
  • Gentle cleanings for small mouths
  • Dental sealants to prevent cavities on molars
  • Fluoride treatments for developing teeth
  • Education on brushing, flossing, and nutrition
  • Monitoring jaw growth and development
