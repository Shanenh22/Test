Permanent Tooth Replacement

Replace Missing Teeth with Dental Implants

Dental implants are titanium posts placed into the jawbone as artificial tooth roots. The ADA recognizes implants as the closest replacement to natural teeth, with success rates above 95%.

Unlike bridges, implants preserve jawbone density by stimulating natural bone growth and don't require alteration of adjacent healthy teeth. They function, look, and feel like your own teeth.

  • Preserves jawbone health and facial structure
  • Looks and functions like a natural tooth
  • No impact on surrounding healthy teeth
  • Can last a lifetime with proper maintenance
  • Success rate above 95% (per ADA data)
