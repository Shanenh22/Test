Gum Health

Treat and Prevent Gum Disease

Periodontal disease affects nearly half of adults over 30, per the CDC. It ranges from mild gingivitis to severe periodontitis that can destroy bone and cause tooth loss.

Treatment depends on severity — from professional cleanings and improved home care to scaling, root planing, and laser-assisted periodontal treatment.

  • Comprehensive periodontal evaluation
  • Scaling and root planing (deep cleaning)
  • Laser gum therapy
  • Antibiotic treatments
  • Ongoing maintenance programs
  • Patient education on prevention
