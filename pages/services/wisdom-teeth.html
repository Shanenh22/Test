Expert Wisdom Teeth Removal

Wisdom teeth typically emerge between ages 17 and 25. According to the AAOMS, approximately 85% will eventually need removal due to impaction, crowding, or infection risk.

Dr. Viyapon evaluates each case with panoramic X-rays to determine position and relationship to surrounding structures, then recommends the most conservative approach.

  • Panoramic digital X-ray evaluation
  • Local anesthesia and sedation options
  • Treatment of impacted and erupted wisdom teeth
  • Detailed post-operative care instructions
  • Follow-up appointments to monitor healing
